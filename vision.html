Our Vision

Our vision is about People Development and People Empowerment.
I see a church making impact and touching lives positively. I see a
church where people are passionately taught and empowered to be
productive and practical with the Word of God. I see a church that is
strongly united without walls of tribe, ethnicitiy or colour of their skin.
I see a church where everybody is trained to be leaders without titles.
