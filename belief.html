Our Beliefs

1. We believe in the totality of the Word of God.
2. We believe in salvation of man through Jesus Christ.
3. We believe in the death, resurrection, ascension and
return of Jesus Christ.
4. We believe in the rapture of the saints.
5. We believe in holiness and righteousness of man.
6. We believe in heaven and hell.
7. We believe in generous and willing giving-offerings
and tithing.
8. We believe in faith in God through Jesus Christ.
9. We believe in miracles.
10. We believe in healings and divine health.
11. We believe in water baptism by immersion.
12. We believe that Jesus Christ is coming back again
as He promised.
