Lead Pastors of Gods Family Church






Dennis Okeah
Pastor Dennis Okeah is the Founder and General overseer of His Family Global Outreach.
He is known for his passion for God and is a leadership and life coach whose mission
is to empower and develop people. Through the creation of the GFC Outreach Arm he is
responsible for the: GFC Soup Kitchen, Widows Outreach, Orphanage Outreach,
Youth Empowerment Outreach. He is happily married to his wife, Pastor Rita Okeah
and they are blessed with children.

Rita Okeah
Pastor Rita Okeah is passionate about educating the youths and helping them find Jesus
as their saviour. She is a beloved Pastor with a prophetic teaching gift, and through
her page Live Diaries, she is known for crafting real stories that will inspire,
educate, develop, empower, motivate, inform and correct! She is the Author of several
books namely, “The Unloved Wife”, “My Pastor, My Love”, “Ripples of Fate”,
“Deceitful webs” and “Diary of a Naija Street Kid”.
